Assignment 2.2 : Create 3 user personas with their goals  , pain points , behavior , how will they use the application and problems.


Persona 1 – Priya Sharma (Patient)

Name: Priya Sharma
Age: 34
Profession: Marketing Manager
Location: Ahmedabad, Gujarat

Goals :
Book doctor appointments quickly
Track health reports in one place
Consult doctors online during busy schedules
Receive reminders for medicines and health checkups

Pain Points :
Too many healthcare apps for different tasks
Confusing medical terms in reports
Long appointment booking processes
Forgetting medicine schedules because of workload

Behavior : 
Uses smartphone daily for work and shopping
Comfortable with digital payments and apps
Prefers clean and fast interfaces
Uses apps mostly at night after work

How Priya Uses HealthSync :

Priya uses HealthSync to:
Book appointments within a few clicks
Attend telehealth consultations from home
Store lab reports digitally
Get AI-powered health summaries in simple language
Receive smart health reminders and wellness tips

Key Problems for Priya :
Managing healthcare with a busy work schedule
Understanding technical health reports
Switching between multiple healthcare apps

Persona 2 – Dr. Raj Mehta (Doctor)

Name: Dr. Raj Mehta
Age: 48
Profession: Cardiologist
Location: Mumbai, Maharashtra

Goals :
Manage patient appointments efficiently
Access patient history quickly
Write prescriptions faster
Reduce administrative workload

Pain Points:
Slow hospital management systems
Too many notifications and patient records
Time wasted during manual prescription writing
Difficulty handling both online and offline consultations

Behavior :
Uses laptop and tablet during work
Prefers systems that save time
Works with multiple patients throughout the day
Needs quick-access dashboards and organized workflows

How Dr. Mehta Uses HealthSync :

Dr. Mehta uses HealthSync to:
Manage schedules and appointments
Conduct telehealth consultations
Access patient records instantly
Use voice-to-text prescription writing
Receive alerts about patient reports and appointments

Key Problems for Dr. Mehta :
Handling heavy patient workload
Maintaining fast consultation speed
Managing multiple healthcare systems together

Persona 3 – Anita Verma (Caregiver)

Name: Anita Verma
Age: 42
Profession: School Teacher
Location: Jaipur, Rajasthan

Goals :
Monitor her elderly father’s health
Track medicine schedules and appointments
Access reports remotely
Get alerts during emergencies

Pain Points :
Managing multiple family health records
Forgetting appointment dates
Difficulty understanding medical updates
Stress during emergency situations

Behavior :
Uses apps mainly for communication and payments
Moderate technical knowledge
Prefers simple navigation and reminders
Frequently checks notifications

How Anita Uses HealthSync :
Anita uses HealthSync to:
Manage multiple family profiles
Monitor vital reports remotely
Set medicine and appointment reminders
Book telehealth consultations for her father
Share reports directly with doctors

Key Problems for Anita :
Managing healthcare responsibilities alone
Organizing reports and prescriptions
Ensuring timely medication and consultations
