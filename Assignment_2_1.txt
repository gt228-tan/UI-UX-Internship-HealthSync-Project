Assignment 2.1 : Describe the main users for the HealthSync Application

HealthSync is designed for multiple types of users involved in healthcare services. Each user has different needs, frustrations, and goals.
Main User Types

Patients
People who want to book appointments, consult doctors online, track health records, and order medicines.

Doctors
Medical professionals who manage patient appointments, prescriptions, consultation history, and schedules.

Caregivers
Family members who manage healthcare activities for elderly parents or children.

Rural / Elderly Users
Users with low digital literacy who need simple interfaces, larger text, and multilingual support.

Admin / Platform Managers
People responsible for managing analytics, doctor approvals, complaints, and overall platform operations.
