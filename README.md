# HealthSync UX Project

## Figma File : https://www.figma.com/design/cytVnlP48kyyjLjiIC3FbZ/HealthSync?node-id=0-1&t=grZUUqE9zjIFkt25-1

## Project Overview

HealthSync is a healthcare platform concept designed to simplify access to medical services for patients, doctors, caregivers, and platform managers. It focuses on:

- Appointment booking and management
- Telehealth consultations
- Digital storage and summarization of health reports
- Medicine reminders and health tracking
- Accessibility for rural and elderly users

The project is primarily a UX and product-definition portfolio for the HealthSync application.

## Key Insights

### Problem Statement
HealthSync addresses common healthcare pain points:

- Complex and confusing appointment booking flows
- Fragmented health information across apps and documents
- Medical jargon that makes reports hard to understand
- Poor accessibility for users with low digital literacy or limited devices
- Low engagement for users tracking their health over time

### Value Proposition
Users choose HealthSync because it offers:

- One-platform access to appointments, reports, telehealth, and medicines
- AI-powered summaries for easier health record comprehension
- Faster, more intuitive appointment and prescription flows
- Notifications and reminders to support better health habits


## User Types

The HealthSync concept targets several user groups:

- Patients
- Doctors
- Caregivers
- Rural / elderly users
- Admin / platform managers

## Personas

Three primary personas are defined for the product:

1. **Priya Sharma** — a busy urban patient who needs fast booking, digital reports, and simple health summaries.
2. **Dr. Raj Mehta** — a clinician who wants appointment efficiency, quick access to patient history, and reduced admin work.
3. **Anita Verma** — a caregiver managing family healthcare, medication reminders, and remote access to medical information.
